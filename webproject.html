About Us

History

Founded in 2010 by Jonathan Rodas, Artisan Saltines is dedicated to producing the highest quality saltines while remaining steadfast in our commitment to reduce hunger. The saltine cracker, a beloved snack that has been a part of countless childhoods, is at the heart of our mission. At Artisan Saltines, we aim to offer a premium selection of crackers that elevate the classic experience, satisfying the desire for a more refined snack. What began as a company offering only our signature saltines has grown into a more dynamic brand, expanding our product range to include a variety of inventive flavors, as well as our delicious Oyster Crackers. As we continue to grow, we are committed to making a positive impact on the world by donating our products to those in need and utilizing sustainable energy in the production of our iconic crackers.

Why Use Us?

At Artisan Saltines, we distinguish ourselves through our forward-thinking and innovative approach to sustainability, offering premium, eco-friendly products that not only meet the highest standards of quality but also contribute to the well-being of our planet. Our dedicated and highly skilled team is committed to delivering exceptional value and service, always placing our customers' needs and satisfaction at the forefront of everything we do. Furthermore, we take immense pride in our corporate social responsibility efforts, pledging 15% of our profits to support initiatives that combat's hunger across the globe. By partnering with us, you are not only choosing a brand that prioritizes environmental stewardship and social impact but also helping to lead the charge toward a brighter, more sustainable future for all.
